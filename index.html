    Legal-Insight

    LegalInsight aims to simplify complex legal documents by creating concise summaries that make legal information more accessible and easy to understand. The project also includes an interactive Q&A feature, allowing users to ask questions about these documents or their summaries. Through this tool, users receive clear responses that highlight relevant sections or define any difficult legal terms, making the reading process easier. The tool will feature a user-friendly interface designed to support individuals of all ages in navigating complex legal texts. By combining both summarization and interactive guidance, LegalInsight provides a reliable resource for understanding important legal content.
